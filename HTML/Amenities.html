Leisure Facilities

Pools

Spend your days relaxing by the pools whilst sipping our refreshing cocktails and lounging in the sun.

Our blissful pool area is an escape from reality. The whole family is welcome at the Seafront Hotel pools, with shallow pools especially for the little ones.

Spa

We invite you to visit our Spa and Indoor Pool area, we promise you will not be disappointed.

Our staff are fully qualifed to ensure you relax as much as possible during your time at the spa.

Seafront Fitness Center

Our Fitness Center boasts high-end gym equipment which we always guarantee to be in their best condition.

When at the Center, our fitness instructors will always be present to guide you, give you tips and tricks, and do what's best for you.

A destination for both travellers and locals, our gym is one of the most recognized on the island, so if you're looking to keep fit during your stay, Seafront Fitness Center is the Place to be.
